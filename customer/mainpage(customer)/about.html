About Our shop




Welcome to Gulamomo Bakery and Cafe, where delicious treats meet warm hospitality. We are passionate about crafting the finest baked goods and providing a cozy atmosphere for our valued customers. Our journey began in 2013 when Syaza Nor Ayu Abdul Zaki opened the doors to our charming bakery in kampung Datuk Keramat . Since then, we've been dedicated to serving mouthwatering pastries, aromatic coffee, and more. At Gulamomo Bakery, we take pride in using only the freshest, high-quality ingredients in our recipes. Our talented bakers pour their heart and soul into every creation, ensuring each bite is a delightful experience. Whether you're stopping by for a quick coffee or celebrating a special occasion with our custom cakes, we aim to exceed your expectations every time you visit.
